Have you ever had a simulation idea with an application ready to go, but forgot what all the PBS options were? Yeah, us too.

HPC-GUI guides you through choosing:

  • Queue and job names
  • Notification settings
  • Number of nodes / tasks per node

HPC-GUI can handle common parallelization regimes for some simulation packages, but you can use it for non-simulation purposes, as well.

Choose a starting point from the menu and submit your jobs from here!
